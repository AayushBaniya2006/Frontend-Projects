Welcome to Lucky Shrub

About Us:

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of".

Origin Story:

Lucky Shrub, founded by the plant-loving couple Jason and Maria, is a thriving nursery and online store born from their shared lifelong passion for plants. They've cultivated a community around quality, sustainability, and expert guidance, inspiring others to join them in the joy of plant care.

Our Roles:

Jason is the garden architect, overseeing design and execution, while managing a team of landscapers. Maria handles marketing and nursery operations, leading branding efforts and ensuring top-quality plants and guidance. Together, they drive Lucky Shrub's success.
